My Paradox Tier List

Who is Paradox Interactive?

Paradox Interactive AB is a video game publisher based in Stockholm, Sweden. Paradox is best known for releasing historically themed strategy video games, especially grand strategy games, and has published strategy games in different settings, as well as games of other genres such as role-playing video games and management simulators. They typically continue development of their games after the initial release with the creation of downloadable content, and are also known for creating games that are easy to mod.

Paradox Grand Strategies I Have Played (Sorted by Starting Game Date)

Crusader Kings 3

Crusader Kings III is a grand strategy role-playing video game set in the Middle Ages. The game starts in 867 and usually ends in the 15th century, showcasing the medieval world and illustrating how feudalism and diplomacy worked. This game is unique among Paradox grand strategy games because you don't control a country; instead, you control a character, a ruler who manages titles like empires, kingdoms, duchies, or baronies. It is one of the most chaotic games on the list due to the randomness of NPC actions, but this only makes the game more replayable.

Europa Universalis 4

Europa Universalis IV is a strategy game where players can control a nation from the Late Middle Ages through the early modern period, engaging in trade, administration, diplomacy, colonization, and warfare. The game is divided into four ages: Age of Discovery, Age of Reformation, Age of Absolutism, and Age of Revolution. Each age has its own special mechanics and events (like the Burgundian inheritance in the Age of Discovery). This game is challenging for new players, but, unlike some other games on this list, all main activities in this game feel complete and interesting.

Conclusion
